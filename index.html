Our mission

Celebrating every kind of mind.

We exist to shift the world's perspective — from seeing neurodiversity as something to be corrected, to something to be celebrated.

More Like Noah Foundation empowers neurodiverse children and their families — including those affected by autism, ADHD, and related conditions — by providing access to resources, education, and community support, while celebrating the unique brilliance of every kind of mind.

🤝
Community & Support
Connecting neurodiverse families with resources, advocacy, and a community that truly understands their journey.
🎓
Education & Awareness
Raising public awareness and promoting inclusion of neurodiverse individuals in all areas of life.
💻
Digital Inclusion
Empowering underserved individuals through tech programs, events, and career pathways in technology.
✨
Celebration
Every child's strengths deserve to be seen, honored, and amplified — not changed.
What we do

Programs & initiatives.

From community events to tech education, every program we run puts neurodiverse children and families first.

🌿
Family Support Events

Community gatherings, resource fairs, and support circles where neurodiverse families can connect, share, and thrive together.

💡
Tech Access Program

Workshops and events helping underserved individuals break into technology careers — building futures one skill at a time.

🎮
143SMP Gaming Events

Community gaming events through our Minecraft server where proceeds and donations go directly to supporting our mission.
